Investigation of Sliding Mode Consensus (SMC) and Integral Sliding Mode Consensus (ISMC) of multi-agent systems

1. The proposed protocol has complete robustness due to rejecting the disturbances and eliminating the reaching phase.
2. There not exist the singularity problem in the developed control law, since the fractional power items of the constructed ISM function can be eliminated in the integral operation.
3. The adaptive continuous ISMC protocol is investigated, and the chattering of the system is reduced.
